Who We Are

Bulsho Kaab is a premier cybersecurity academy dedicated to training the next generation of ethical hackers, security analysts, and cyber defenders. We believe in hands-on, practical education using real-world tools.

Our courses are built around the most popular open-source cybersecurity tools from GitHub, giving students direct experience with the same tools used by professionals.
